CCTM

The Camera Centering Tripod Mount is built on an Arduino base. It scans a room and finds the closest object. Then a camera is moved in front of the closest object, after which the picture is taken.

STRIDE

STRIDE stands for Simple Technological Reconnaissance Interface for Distance Estimation. Basically, STRIDE is a device that alerts visually impaired users of objects they may not see in close proximity as they are walking so that they can take further precautions against it.

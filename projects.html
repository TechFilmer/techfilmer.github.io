CCTM

The Camera Centering Tripod Mount is built on Arduino. It scans a room and finds the closest object. Then a camera is moved in front of the closest object, after which the picture is taken.

PyMS

PyMS stands for Python Music Server, because that's what it is. PyMS uses a client-server architecture to play music or other audio across a network.

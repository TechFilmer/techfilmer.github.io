Projects

While I have worked on many projects, my four largest projects are:

STRIDE

STRIDE is a wearable for the visually impaired that detects objects in front of them, by using ultrasonic sensors and pressure sensors, to help prevent falls. I developed the whole project, which involved the creation of a PCB, the outer-casing, and the
Winner of the Fairfax County 50+ Assistive Technology Shark Tank Competition

Ignite a Mind

Ignite a Mind is a non-profit startup I have co-founded that aims to bring information to offline villages. If the Internet cannot be brought to villages, why not bring at least the information on the Internet?
3rd Place at Startup Weekend DC: Social Impact

Fi(n)do

Fi(n)do was a project at Bitcamp that identifies objects and outputs their name through headphones to help blind people all while following the user. This project integrated Raspberry Pi, OpenCV, Image Recognition, Robotics, and Text-to-Speech Synthesis.

Arduino Class Development

I teach Arduino 101 classes at a local makerspace (Nova Labs) to both young kids and adults. For this class I developed the curriculum, and the projects.

Some of my other projects are CCTM, IdeaVine, Nymeria.js, python-polr, Simplyfi, nlkickstarter, and Gurudev: The Musical.
